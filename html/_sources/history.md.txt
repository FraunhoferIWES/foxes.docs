# v0.1.0-alpha
This is the initial release of **foxes** - ready for testing.

So far not many models have been transferred from the Fraunhofer IWES in-house predecessor _flappy_, they will be added in the following versions. Also optimization is not yet included. We are just getting started here!

Enjoy - we are awaiting comments and issues, thanks for testing.

**Full Changelog**: https://github.com/FraunhoferIWES/foxes/commits/v0.1.0
